# vs-ai2.38: robust space — space that survives the worst cut

## Overview

The bot opens from an empty board with a width-1 diagonal tendril
(10,10/9,9/8,8 …) that races to the center. Every cell of that filament is an
articulation point, so one cut by the opponent forfeits the whole distal chain
and the game. The space-race eval term (`spaceRaceWeight=32`, `evaluate.go`)
pays ~222/cell for deepest-first Voronoi expansion; the diagonal is
Voronoi-optimal, so the eval loves it and nothing prices its fragility in the
opening.

Attempt 1 (a competing ungated own-cutLoss PENALTY term) was a null result: no
weight flips the opening without wrecking strength (see the in-code note at the
top of `evaluate.go` and `docs/plans/completed/20260717-vs-ai2.38-ungated-fragility.md`).
DO NOT retry a penalty term.

**This attempt — ROBUST SPACE: change what the space term COUNTS, not add a
rival term.** Seed each player's space-race BFS not from their full connected
territory, but only from the component that SURVIVES their own worst single-cell
cut. Concretely: find the player's max-`cutLoss` articulation cell (already
computed in `articulationPointsInto` → `workspace.cutLoss`), hypothetically
remove it, and seed the Voronoi partition only from the surviving,
base-containing component. A width-1 tendril then collapses to ~the base and
claims ~zero deep space; a width-2 front has no single cut point so it keeps its
full body and full space credit. Same units (cells), NO new weight to tune —
`spaceRaceWeight` stays 32. Applied symmetrically to every active player.

## Technical Details

- `cutLoss[i]` semantics: DFS is rooted at the base, so subtree sizes measure
  cells separated FROM the base when `i` is removed — exactly the loss the
  robust seed must exclude. Max over articulation cells = worst single cut.
- Symmetry: every active player's seed is shrunk the same way from the same
  position in the same shared BFS, so `spaceRace`'s side-to-move-independence
  and tie/contested handling are preserved (opponent fragile space is also
  discounted, which aligns with the existing predatory cut bonus at lines
  ~212-228).
- Cost: one extra base-BFS per active player per eval (O(cells)); affordable
  under the 1000ms budget after the vs-ai2.36 Position-path speedups.
